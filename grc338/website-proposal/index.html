Introduction

Abalone Surf Shop

Abalone Surf Shop is a family owned and operated surf shop that showcases local artists and local surfboard shapers. They sell hand shaped boards, apparel, and all the high quality surf gear and accessories you could need.

Target audience

The people that use this site are people who want to browse and shop for surf gear, apparel, or boards. They are usually local surfers who don't mind spending money on high quality items such as hand shaped surfboards. They also could be either locals or tourists who enjoy the the look and design of their apparel and wanted to order some online.

Their primary tasks and goals are usually to browse or online shop to order something. However, vistors may just be checking out the website to find the shop's address or contact information as well.

About

Abalone Surf Shop is a family owned and operated surf shop, but we're more than that. Abalone has become a place to gather, to show art and to meet people as well as to buy boards and other surf stuff. We focus on building a community of like minded people who like to make things, go places, and have good times. Stop on by the shop to say hello, you never know what kind of antics you'll get into.

  • 6'0" Christian Beamish Old School Twin

    $960

    "Old School Twins" break into two groups: 6'4" and under, and 6'6" and up. The shorter Twinnies are whip-sharp and fast, with surprising hold and drive when the surfer lays into turns with heavily planted feet and a buried rail. If the boards are surfed in a "flicky" manner, they tend to skate and slide (which can be fun and have its place), but they come alive when a rider gets over the board with his or her weight and drives hard.

  • 9'4" Elmore Sam's Club

    $1535

    The Sam's Club is the perfect all-around log with a "Sam-style" outline. It has a slightly pulled in nose and tail block, yet a nice middle width. It has a gentle nose concave that transcends into a gentle roll through the middle and out the tail, allowing for a multifaceted longboard that turns well off the tail and provides stability on the nose. This is a versatile log that works great regardless of the style of wave you surf consistently (beach, point, reef, etc.)
