Boards

We carry a large selection of surfboards to fit a wide variety of surfing conditions

Shortboards

  • 5′10″ Mitsven B Tail - $1165

    The B-tail thruster has the speed and planning of a fish surfboard but is much more fluid and maneuverable as well as much better performing top to bottom on the face of the wave. This model features a wider nose, relaxed rocker, a single to double concave bottom contour, and Mitsven’s own rail style. The result is a super fast, user friendly board that performs way better than your fish.

  • 5′2″ Mistven Fish - $1295

    We asked Bob Mitsven to send us a couple twin keels with pulled in tails for maneuverability in tight spaces. The rails are slightly turned up, offering a delicious edge for bottom and top turns. These boards will work in a variety of conditions and will have you grinning when the waves are sub-par or straight firing!

  • 5′8″ Furrow Olivella - $1185

    A wide nose round pintail model. Named after the Olivella shells that cover our local coastline in the winter, the Olivella is a fast, little pocket board that loves mushy chop to lined up clean waves.

  • 5′9″ Tyler Warren Dream Fish - $1245

    Influenced by the San Diego fish design. A classic replica with a modern foil and feel. This board is fast, loose and takes you back to the basics. Speed, trim and maneuverability.

  • 6′2″ Radio Plane Jane - $755

    Radio Surfboards are master designs created by Allan Gibbons for Mollusk. This one is a fast drivey thruster for sub-par to good days in the waist-to-head high range. Fun in the pocket or even hunting down an air section. Guaranteed speed and drive throughout turns will have you questioning if this board is really even a plane jane in the first place.

  • 6′6″ Weston Guppy - $940

    Ahhh, the long fish. The agility of a fish with a lot more trim and paddle power. Long fish are a shop favorite.

Not your ordinary jelly bean!

  • 6′6″ Mitsven Tri Fin Swallow - $1210

  • 7′8″ Mitsven Egg - $1240

It's a stripped down business orientated board for people concerned with trim and engaging the rail. This board is 100% hand built with predominate down rails, it still has a good amount of up turn making for well defined sharp edge.

  • 9′2″ Tyler Warren Transistion - $1830
